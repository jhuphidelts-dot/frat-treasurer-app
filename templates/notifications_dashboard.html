Pending Approval Requests
This is where approval notifications work:
  • When a brother submits a payment plan request
  • When a chair submits a reimbursement request
  • When someone submits a spending plan request
  • Any other requests that need treasurer approval
How it works:
  1. Brother/Chair submits request through the app
  2. You get instant SMS + email notification
  3. You review and approve/deny in the app
  4. Submitter gets notified of your decision
Note: This is different from payment reminders to brothers about their dues. Those are handled through Selective Reminders.
